Fun Facts About Monkeys

Monkeys are fascinating creatures known for their intelligence, agility, and playful behavior. They are found in tropical forests, savannas, and even mountains!

Monkeys are curious and highly social animals!

Did You Know?

    Monkeys have unique fingerprints—just like humans! Some species can use tools to gather food and crack nuts. A group of monkeys is called a "troop." Capuchin monkeys wash their hands and feet with urine to mark territory. Monkeys express emotions through facial expressions, sounds, and body movements.
